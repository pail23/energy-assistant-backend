Energy Assistant

Energy Assistant is a free, opensource Energy Management System which interfaces with Home Assistant and Evcc. Home Assistant is a pre-requisit to run Energy Assistant. Evcc is optional. In order to use evcc, the Evcc Homeassistant component is mandatory to be installed in Home Assistant.

Energy Assistant is using Emhass in order to optimize the energy consumption of your house.
